# Privacy Policy for Memolio

_Last updated: 20 June 2026_

**Memolio** is an Android digital picture-frame app that turns a tablet into an always-on photo frame. It is designed to be **fully offline**. This policy explains what data the app does and does not handle. The short version: Memolio does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data or your photos.

## 1. Information we collect

**None.** Memolio does not collect personal information. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, no crash-reporting SDKs, and no usage tracking of any kind.

## 2. Your photos

Photos you add to Memolio are stored **only on your own device**, inside the app's private storage. They are never uploaded to us or to any cloud service. When you send photos to the frame from another device's web browser, they travel **directly over your local Wi-Fi network** to the app — they do not leave your network and they never pass through our servers (we operate none).

## 3. Network use

To receive photo uploads, the app runs a small web server **on your local network only**. This server is reachable solely by devices on the same Wi-Fi and is protected by a secret token.

The app makes **one** kind of outbound internet connection: when you choose to purchase or restore the optional "Pro" upgrade, it contacts the billing services described in Section 5 to verify your purchase. After that, the unlock is cached on the device and the app continues to work offline. No other internet connection is made.

## 4. Permissions

Memolio does not request broad photo or media permissions. It manages its own private storage area and does not read the rest of the device's photo library.

## 5. Purchases (optional "Pro" upgrade)

The one-time Pro upgrade is processed through **Google Play Billing**, with purchase validation handled by **RevenueCat**. We never see or store your payment details. To validate your entitlement, a randomly generated app-user identifier and your purchase receipt/token are processed by these services. We use this solely to confirm whether the Pro upgrade is active — not to identify or track you. Their handling of this data is governed by their own policies:

- Google Play / Google: [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- RevenueCat: [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

## 6. Data sharing and sale

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. The only data leaving your device is the purchase information in Section 5, and only at the moment you buy or restore the Pro upgrade.

## 7. Children

Memolio does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is suitable for general audiences and contains no ads and no data collection.

## 8. Data retention and deletion

Because your content stays on your device, you control it entirely. Deleting a photo in the app moves it to a Recently-Deleted area and removes it permanently after 30 days. Uninstalling the app removes all of its data from the device.
